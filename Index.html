About

The Computer Club is a vibrant community that focuses on all things related to technology and computers. It is a gathering of students who share a common interest in exploring and expanding their knowledge of the digital world. Through the club, members have the opportunity to engage in various activities such as coding. Our supportive mentors will guide you every step of the way, helping you develop important skills like teamwork and leadership. By joining the club, you'll gain valuable knowledge, make new friends, and prepare yourself for future opportunities in the ever-changing world of technology. Get ready to unleash your inner tech wizard with the Computer Club!

Club Info

Welcome to the Computer Club at S.M.K Jalan Kebun, a place where the world of technology comes alive! Our primary focus is to provide students with a platform to expand their knowledge and skills in the exciting realm of computers. We offer various learning opportunities to help students delve deeper into the world of technology.

Computer Club also provides numerous opportunities for students to showcase their talents and creativity. From participating in coding competitions to developing innovative software applications, students have the chance to demonstrate their skills and stand out in the field of technology.
